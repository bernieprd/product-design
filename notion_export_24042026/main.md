## About me

I’ve been a Product Designer for 9+ years. Currently, I’m at Rover, where I work on the sitter side of the marketplace — from search and retention to alternative monetization models. My favourite problems live on the supply side: building tools that sitters, couriers, and operators use every day to run their business.

Before that, I spent time at Glovo, Stuart, and Guestcentric, working on logistics, last‑mile delivery, and hotel management systems.

I’m a big believer in end‑to‑end ownership: understanding users and the business, exploring problems and solutions with a cross‑functional team, navigating technical constraints together, and measuring the impact of what we actually deliver.

*Off-duty, I’m likely running by the beach* 🏖️
