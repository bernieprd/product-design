I've been a Product Designer for 9+ years. Currently at Rover, where I work on the sitter side of the marketplace — from search and retention to alternative monetisation models.

My favourite problems live on the supply side: building tools that sitters, couriers, and operators use every day to run their business.

Before Rover, I spent time at Glovo, Stuart, and GuestCentric, working on logistics, last-mile delivery, and hotel management systems.

I'm a big believer in end-to-end ownership: understanding users and the business, exploring problems and solutions with a cross-functional team, navigating technical constraints together, and measuring the impact of what we actually deliver.
